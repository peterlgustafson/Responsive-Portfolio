About Me

Born and raised in Chicago. After studying Economics and International Affairs at George Mason University in the Washington, DC area I moved to Seattle and worked at 2 early stage SAAS startups which have both been recently acquired. I started in Customer Support and worked my way up into Account Management, Sales Engineering and Business Development roles.

After 3 years in Seattle I moved back to Chicago and worked for Trading Technologies in a Customer Success Operations role helping them build out process and systems to migrate their existing client base onto their new cloud based platform. In January 2018 I resigned by position and started a Full Stack Web Development bootcamp at Northwestern University. I am excited to learn how to code and take the next step in my career!
